Sub Therapy

Sub Therapy

Sub Therapy envisions electronic music as a space for shared sensory experience, going beyond mere track selection to create immersive and evolving journeys.

Her sets, whether aimed at the club or ambient contexts, are guided by a common intention: to align body and listening. Speed is never an end in itself. Dance can be both physical and introspective, directed toward the dancefloor as much as toward an inner landscape. Over time, a collective dynamic emerges — a common ground shaped by attention, presence, and connection.

Rooted in club culture without reproducing its expected roughness, Sub Therapy's sound has a distinctly organic quality. Synthetic textures open onto breathing spaces, melodic expanses, and shifting rhythms. Genres are treated as thresholds rather than fixed identities, allowing fluid movement between deep, hypnotic, and atmospheric territories.

Refusing easy effects and predictable builds, she lets tracks fully unfold, approaching them with restraint and precision. Technique serves listening, never the other way around. Her sets are built less on impact than on progression — a continuous response to what the music reveals.

In ambient contexts, time stretches and deepens, with an emphasis on detail, texture, and subtlety of perception. In more rhythmic environments, energy remains controlled and fluid, allowing bodies to move freely, without excess.

The boundary between guide and guided dissolves. The crowd influences the direction as much as she does, creating a feedback loop where movement, attention, and emotion shape the experience.

Sub Therapy's approach turns toward an open future, creating spaces where music is not an escape, but a form of presence: collective, immersive, and transformative.

C O N T E N T

C O N T E N T

No rest when you're in sync

Booking

Partners in life as well as behind the decks, Sub Therapy and Modestie are CONTENT.

Roaming Paris dancefloors for nearly a decade, their sensibility and feel for the crowd merge through a refined selection. Contagious energy and mischievous glances. Let the alchemy run for a few hours to get the desired effect.

Whether lion or libra, cages open, faces light up and t-shirts sometimes come off… Often.

The stages that have hosted them still remember it. From La Station to Concrete, from the Alter Paname stages to Nipples, Roue Libre or Microclimat, the magic hasn't stopped in recent years. Even the scorpions stood up…

From private parties to raves for the initiated, they've operated with sets that defy the contemporary clock. No inhibitions, just love to give — our very own Mickey and Mallory Knox.

Indestructible thrill-seekers, born and built for trance-figured dancefloors.

Modestie

Modestie

Booking

After mixing on the Great Wall of China at the YinYang Festival, Modestie returned to France determined to dig deeper and take the craft more seriously.

Starting with the few records gathered during his travels, he gradually expanded his collection, deeply influenced by the KOMPAKT label, collecting sunny gems through countless hours of listening in record shops.

He cut his teeth in Paris with a residency at the Garage, playing at Concrete, and riding the decks of some of the city's most underground collectives — Péripate, Nipples, Microclimat, La Toilette, and Positive Altitude. It was during some of these events that he formed the duo C O N T E N T with Sub Therapy.

The abrupt halt of nightlife in 2020 led him to discover and learn more about electronic music, diving deep into discographies from the 80s and 90s: New Beat, Techno, sometimes trancy, or ambient. An endless range of styles he explored to refine his playlists and ignite dancefloors — whether sweaty, uninhibited, or more laid-back.
